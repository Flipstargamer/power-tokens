Power Tokens provide token items which can be used to gain positive and negative powers. This creates a fun and new way to play Minecraft.

**Press [V] to open up a list of your powers.**

# Powers
Powers are random passive effects you have that are permentant till you wipe them with a **Inverted Token**. They are split into two categories: buffs and debuffs.
### Buffs
* **More Health** - Gain 5 more hearts.
* **Speed Boost** - Gain a 60% speed boost.
* **Tiny** - Become 50% smaller.
* **Natural Armor** - Gain a passive 2.5 armor points
* **Miner Training** - Mine 100% faster.
* **Swift Hands** - Attacks take less time to charge up.
* **Less Knockback** - Attacks deal 50% less knockback.
* **Lung Capacity** - You have more air while under water.
### Debuffs
* **Less Health** - Loose 5 hearts.
* **Flammable** - Burn for longer.
* **Weakened Defenses** - Passively loose 2.5 armor points.
* **More Knockback** - Deal 50% more knockback.
* **Heavy Hands** - Attacks take more time to charge up.
* **Less Damage** - Deal less damage.
* **Big** - Become 30% bigger.
* **Less Speed** - Become 30% slower.
* **Metal Bones** - Take fall damage 1 block earlier.
* **Aquaphobic** - Take damage while touching water.
* **Vampire** - Burn when in broad daylight.

# Power Tokens
To gain a power you must first craft one of the power tokens. There are three different types of power tokens.
* Unstable Token - Cheapest, however provides two debuff powers for a random buff power.
* Power Token - Provides a random buff power alongside a random debuff power.
* Reinforced Token - Provides a random buff power only.
* Selective Token -  Allows you to pick one power. **Uncraftable**

## Reseting
You may be unsatisfied with your debuffs, so you can craft an **Inverted Token**. to remove all your powers. However, this includes all your buffs.
